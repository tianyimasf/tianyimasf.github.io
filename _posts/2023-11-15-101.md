What is the meaning of this?

Some background information first.

Growing up, especially as a girl in a collectivism culture, I was expected to fit in. I wanted people to like me so so much. Even though I maintain a cool front, I felt bad every time I look at the “popular” kids and thought that I’m not one of them.

I’m not one of the “normal” kids.

The normal range of personality is 0-100, and I’m the 100 plus 1. You can even tell from the way I numericalized the range: I started from index 0, like a programmer, instead of 1, like a normal dude. (I go with any pronouns btw, shocking! Bet not anyone just goes around and say that.)

I didn’t feel that that’s okay for most of my life. Not until I met soon-to-be my one of the most important person in my life -- my now husband, whose instagram bio is: I found solitude in an act of harmless peculiarity.

Hmm...there’s something about this dude that I like 😉

In a lot of ways, him and me are very similar: we both view work as part of life, instead of all of life. We like idle and slow times. We say we don’t have any IRL friends 😄, and there is some truth to that.

We are the minority.

And I became used to it. And gradually and unintentionally, accepted it.

Even though the majority of the people are going in one direction, it’s okay to create and enjoy your own journey. Unique to you.

Since this realization, I started to see more and more people, just walking their own path in life, different from what seems to be the mainstream, but equally meaningful and enriching.

So I want to let others who might be also struggling with this know that it’s ok to choose what You think, believe and do, despite what others are doing.

It’s ok to be the 100 plus 1.
